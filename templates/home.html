What is Funguide?

Funguide utilizes a deep learning model to identify a mushroom's genus from an uploaded image. The model was trained with transfer learning and a dataset containing images of 9 different genera of mushrooms.

Funguide is meant to identify the following mushroom genera:

  • Agaricus
  • Amanita
  • Boletus
  • Cortinarius
  • Entoloma
  • Hygrocybe
  • Lactarius
  • Russula
  • Suillus
Upload an image of a mushroom and Funguide will predict its genus
